##overview
LSC Access is an accessibility project focused on improving access to digital media for deaf and mute people through the transcription of Spanish text into Colombian sign language (LSC).

##problem
Most government or institutional websites and applications do not include accessibility for users with hearing and communication impairments, lacking sign language for reading their content.

##Why it matters
Digital accessibility is absolutely essential. This project focuses on reducing barriers to communication and access to information and promoting the inclusion of the deaf community.


##Current goal
Create a browser extension connected to an API that converts text into LSC-friendly
visual content.

##Project status.
Project under development - research and prototyping phase.
